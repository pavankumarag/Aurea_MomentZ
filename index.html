What's in the name

The name Aurea in latin means GOLD.Every event we are part of is a golden moment for our customer, we help them to remember the special occassion for a very long time with our unique way of service

Dictionary: Aurea is a Latin female name meaning "golden".Meaning and name origin: Aurea \a(u)-rea\ as a girl's name is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Aurea is "Golden; Golden-haired; gold; Wind; Dawn; breeze". Aurea is a variant of Aura (Latin); mythology: Aura was the goddess of breezes.
